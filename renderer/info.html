How to Use

  • 📊 The app monitors your CPU usage in real-time
  • 😊 Your character's emotion changes based on CPU load
  • ⚙️ Right-click the tray icon to access Settings
  • 🎨 Choose your favorite character in Settings
  • ➕ Add custom characters with your own images

Emotion States

😌 Calm (0-25%)
🙂 Normal (25-55%)
😄 Excited (55-80%)
😤 Stressed (80%+)
